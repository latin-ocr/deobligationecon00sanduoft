23. The THIRD Doum* is, What sort and what amount of consent
on the part of the People is requisite, in order that the Will of the
Prince may have the force of Law.

We may consider in this, two points, the Time and the Manner of
this consent: the Time, as to whether the consent is to be before or
after the passing of the Law: the Blannem as to whether the consent
is given expressly, by votes, or tacitly, by custom. And thus there are
four Degrees of Popular Consent.
