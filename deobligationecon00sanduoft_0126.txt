gedians. If there were not such inflictions, nations would be happy
in proportion as they were out of the reach of the Grospel, for then
they would have no sin, Rom. iv. 15, 1 John iii. 4. But Conscience

is manifest and conspicuous among the heathen. Therefore Scriptura
